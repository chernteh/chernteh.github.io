Market-News Bot

Guarding an Unattended LLM Against Confident Nonsense

A fully automated daily market brief delivered over Telegram. A two-model LLM pipeline (Llama 3.3 70B + Llama 4 Scout) writes and self-checks each summary, backed by Python guardrails for hallucination prevention and a 4-source price-arbitration layer.
